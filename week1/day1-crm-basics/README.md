# Day 1 - CRM Basics

## What is Salesforce?

Salesforce is a cloud-based CRM platform used by companies to manage customers, sales, support, and business processes efficiently.

---

## What is CRM?

CRM stands for Customer Relationship Management.

It helps companies:

- Store customer information
- Track sales
- Manage communication
- Improve customer relationships

---

## Why Companies Use Salesforce

Companies use Salesforce because it:

- Organizes customer data
- Improves sales tracking
- Automates business processes
- Helps teams collaborate
- Provides cloud access from anywhere

---

# Important Concepts

## Account

An Account represents a company or organization.

Example:
Infosys, TCS, Amazon

---

## Contact

A Contact is a person associated with an Account.

Example:
Manager, customer, employee

---

## Opportunity

Opportunity represents a potential business deal or sale.

Example:
A company planning to buy software services.

---

# Business Workflow

Lead → Contact → Opportunity → Customer

## Explanation

1. Lead
   A person shows interest in a product or service.

2. Contact
   After verification, lead becomes a contact.

3. Opportunity
   A possible business deal is created.

4. Customer
   When deal is successful, customer is finalized.
